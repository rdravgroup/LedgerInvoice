Quick Actions

Update Ageing Buckets Recalculate invoice age

Recalculates the ageing buckets (0-30, 30-60, 60-90, 90+ days) for all invoices.

Schedule: Daily @ 1:00 AM
Refresh Outstanding Summary Recalculate outstanding AR

Refreshes the outstanding summary including total AR, collection rates, and DSO.

Schedule: Daily @ 1:15 AM
Create Ageing Snapshot Archive monthly snapshot

Creates a monthly snapshot of ageing data for historical tracking and trend analysis.

Schedule: 1st of month @ 2:00 AM
About Maintenance Jobs

Automated Schedule

These jobs run automatically on the schedule shown above. You can manually trigger them here if needed.

When to Trigger Manually

  • Before generating important reports
  • During month-end close process
  • After bulk invoice imports
  • When debugging data issues

What Each Job Does

  • Update Ageing Buckets: Recalculates which invoices are past due and updates the ageing buckets
  • Refresh Outstanding: Recalculates total outstanding AR, collection rates, and DSO metrics
  • Create Snapshot: Creates a historical snapshot for trend analysis

info Note: Running these jobs may take some time for large datasets. Please be patient.
